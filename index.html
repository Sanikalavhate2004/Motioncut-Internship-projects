TraVezo is the largest independently owned travel agency headquartered in Mumbai, and consistently ranked among the top 50 largest in the world.

Since 2010 we have been one of the greatest and most efficient travel service company. We are an employee-owned company with a passion for providing the excellent customer service, using our expertise and technology to deliver innovative and comfy travel ideas in a gratifying work environment.
